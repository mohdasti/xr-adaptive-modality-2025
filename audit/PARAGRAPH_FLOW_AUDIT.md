## Main Types of Flow Problems Found

1. **Transition gaps:** One transition was strengthened—the jump from the rigid-dichotomy paragraph to the platform introduction was made explicit with "To investigate whether adaptive support can alleviate these tradeoffs."

2. **Redundancy:** The Gaze Simulation paragraph repeated "precise control over noise characteristics" in consecutive sentences; the second instance was revised to "explicit parametric control" for variety and precision.

3. **Concluding-sentence precision:** The Primary Performance Results paragraph ended with "Hand outperformed gaze on throughput and error rate. @fig-performance illustrates these differences." Since three metrics (throughput, error rate, movement time) were reported, "hand outperformed gaze on throughput and error rate" understated the summary. Revised to "Hand outperformed gaze on all three metrics."

4. **Terminology consistency:** One instance of "adaptive modality systems" in the Discussion (future work paragraph) was changed to "modality-specific adaptive systems" for consistency with the rest of the manuscript.
